Table language

Four moves.
Four clear meanings.

Your legal choices depend on whether another player has already bet on the current street.

F

Fold

Give up the hand. You lose chips already in the pot, but pay nothing more.

K

Check

Pass the action without betting. You can only check when there is no bet to match.

C

Call

Match the current bet and stay in the hand. The betting can then continue or close.

R

Raise

Increase the current bet. Opponents must fold, call, or raise again.
