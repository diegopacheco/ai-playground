AIR BOARD

A whiteboard you draw with your bare hands. Point to draw, thumbs up to change colour, open your hand to erase. Tracks up to two people at once.

YOUR CAMERA

Show your hand to the camera.

GESTURES
  • Point (index) — draw
  • Thumbs up — change colour
  • Open hand (5 fingers) — eraser
  • Fist — pen up

Up to two hands are tracked — a second person can draw alongside you.
