# Games Catalog

A catalog of the games built across the `pocs/` folder — computer-vision games, AI agent games, browser games and simple game POCs.

Website (Games Catalog): [games/index.html](https://diegopacheco.github.io/ai-playground/games/)

## Computer Vision Games (play with your hands, face and voice)

1. **Air Fishing** — A browser fishing game you play with your bare hands: your index finger is the rod, tracked live from the webcam.
   [pocs/air-fishing](https://github.com/diegopacheco/ai-playground/tree/main/pocs/air-fishing)

2. **Air Lango** — A 2-D boxing game you fight with your bare hands, no keyboard, driven by webcam hand tracking.
   [pocs/air-lango-cv](https://github.com/diegopacheco/ai-playground/tree/main/pocs/air-lango-cv)

3. **Air Board** — A browser whiteboard you draw on with your bare hands, no pen and no mouse, the webcam is the input.
   [pocs/dashboard-cv](https://github.com/diegopacheco/ai-playground/tree/main/pocs/dashboard-cv)

4. **Puppet Shadows** — Hand-shadow puppet recognition with OpenCV contour matching: make a shadow puppet and it names the animal in real time.
   [pocs/fable5-puppet-shadows-cv](https://github.com/diegopacheco/ai-playground/tree/main/pocs/fable5-puppet-shadows-cv)

5. **Sound Guesser** — Make a sound into your microphone (a clap, a knock, a whistle) and the AI guesses what it is, live, fully in the browser.
   [pocs/sound-to-guess-cv](https://github.com/diegopacheco/ai-playground/tree/main/pocs/sound-to-guess-cv)

6. **Jurassic Runner** — An endless runner set in an old jungle of the Jurassic age, with no keyboard — played with the webcam.
   [pocs/jurassic-runner-game](https://github.com/diegopacheco/ai-playground/tree/main/pocs/jurassic-runner-game)

7. **Open Can Enduro** — A tiny browser racer in the spirit of Atari's Enduro, with no keyboard — played with the webcam.
   [pocs/open-can-enduro-game](https://github.com/diegopacheco/ai-playground/tree/main/pocs/open-can-enduro-game)
